1.Where was the first olympic game held?

=>Athens,Greece
Although the ancient Games were staged in Olympia, Greece, from 776 BC through 393 AD, it took 1503 years for the Olympics to return. The first modern Olympics were held in Athens, Greece, in 1896.

2.Who is known as the father of modern olympics?

=>Baron Pierre de Coubertin.
Pierre de Frédy, Baron de Coubertin (1 January 1863 – 2 September 1937) was a French educator and historian, and founder of the International Olympic Committee. He is considered the father of the modern Olympic Games.

3.What is the name of oficial mascot of RIO 2016?

=>Vinicius
The name of the Olympic mascot pays tribute to Brazilian musician Vinicius de Moraes.Vinicius is a mix of different Brazilian animals. His design takes inspiration from pop culture, as well as video game and animation characters. Alongside his Paralympic Games colleague, Vinicius represents the diversity of the Brazilian people and culture, as well as its exuberant nature.

4.Which nation has won its gold medal in soccer in the 2016 Olympics?

=>Brazil
The host nation,Brazil won its first Olympic gold medal in soccer with a penalty kick by Neymar to defeat Germany. Neymar converted the decisive penalty in the shootout after Brazilian goalkeeper Weverton stopped Nils Petersen's shot from the spot. Brazil had lost the Olympic final three times. The win means a unified Germany are now the only World Cup winners never to have won the Olympic gold medal.

7.The olympic motto- Citius, Altius, Fortius,What does Altius in the motto means?

=>Higher
The Olympic motto is Citius, Altius, Fortius, which is Latin for "Faster, Higher, Stronger". It was proposed by Pierre de Coubertin upon the creation of the International Olympic Committee in 1894.
